Why EV Battery Recycling Matters

The world is transitioning to electric vehicles, but what happens to their batteries?

The Problem

With millions of EV batteries reaching end-of-life, improper disposal threatens environmental health and wastes valuable materials worth billions.

Our Solution

We provide comprehensive battery servicing, repair, and recycling services to extend battery life and recover precious materials sustainably.

Our Impact

Creating a circular economy for EV batteries while reducing carbon footprint and supporting the global transition to clean energy.

Our Vision for the Future

As electric vehicles become the standard worldwide, battery waste management will be critical infrastructure. EnergyVault is pioneering a sustainable circular economy for EV batteries, ensuring that every battery is recycled responsibly, materials are recovered efficiently, and second-life applications extend product value.

We're committed to supporting the EV industry's growth while protecting our planet. Our technology and expertise will set new standards for battery recycling, creating economic opportunities while solving one of the most pressing environmental challenges of our time.
